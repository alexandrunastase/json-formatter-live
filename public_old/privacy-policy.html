Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how JSON Formatter Live ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from you when you use our website. All JSON formatting and processing is done entirely in your browser, and we do not store or transmit your data to any servers.

Local Storage

We use local storage in your browser to save your theme preference (light or dark mode). This information is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party CDN (Content Delivery Network) services to deliver JavaScript libraries and CSS frameworks. These services may collect some anonymous usage data, such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via GitHub.
